Máscara de Momia en 3D

Práctica de 3D para el Módulo 2 - Tema 5 del Curso de Tecnologías Avanzadas para la Gestión y Documentación del Patrimonio Cultural. 
Docente: Juam Carlos Torres Canteiro.

Painted and gilded cartonnage mummy-mask with a striped lappet-wig;
representation of Khepri on top of the head; 
Hieroglyphic text around the fillet; 
on the back are three painted panels, 
the uppermost depicting Sekhmet, Isis, Horus and Osiris, 
dthe lower two depicting winged deities.
